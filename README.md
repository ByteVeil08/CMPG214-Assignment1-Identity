# CMPG214 – Assignment 1: Personal Identity System

## About Me
My name is Jandré van Zyl and I am currently studying towards a BSc in Information Technology. I enjoy problem solving, working with technology, and doing creative work such as drawing and design.

## About This Assignment
This repository contains my work for Assignment 1 in CMPG214. The goal of the assignment was to design a personal identity system that represents who I am. This included creating a logomark and an infographic that explains aspects of my identity such as what I care about, what I am working toward, and the meaning behind my design.

## Repository Structure
- **logo/** – The final version of my logomark  
- **infographic/** – The final infographic explaining my identity system  
- **process/** – Early logo concepts and design exploration

## Logomark
My logomark combines a geometric eye with my initials (J, V, Z). The eye represents observation and reflection, which connects to how I approach learning, problem solving, and creativity.
